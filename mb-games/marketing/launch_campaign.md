# Launch Marketing Plan
Studio: MAD BITCOINS RETRO GAME LAB
Titles: Bear Market Breakout + Mempool Meltdown + WCN Signal Jam + Mad Meme Blaster + Halving Havoc + Citadel Climb + Node Hopper Drift

## Brand Positioning
- Core message: `Bitcoin meme culture meets authentic retro gameplay.`
- Audience:
  - Retro arcade and NES/Atari enthusiasts
  - Bitcoin and crypto meme communities
  - Streamers who like high-score challenges
  - World Crypto Network and Mad Bitcoins nostalgia audiences

## Product Hooks
### Bear Market Breakout
- Hook: `Break the bear market one sat at a time.`
- Selling points:
  - Immediate arcade action
  - Easy controls, hard mastery
  - Meme-heavy score attack

### Mempool Meltdown
- Hook: `The chain is congested. Think fast or get rekt.`
- Selling points:
  - Strategic puzzle depth
  - Fast replay loops
  - Distinct crypto-themed mechanics

### WCN Signal Jam
- Hook: `Keep the signal alive while jammer fleets flood the sky.`
- Selling points:
  - Fast wave-based action
  - Broadcast meter burst mechanic
  - Strong World Crypto Network thematic tie

### Mad Meme Blaster
- Hook: `Charge the rant meter. Erase the paper-hands formation.`
- Selling points:
  - Classic formation shooter readability
  - Humor-first meme enemy design
  - Big replay value from escalating waves

### Halving Havoc
- Hook: `When the wave doubles, answer with a halving blast.`
- Selling points:
  - Arena survival intensity
  - Charge-and-burst risk management
  - Stream-friendly high-score pacing

### Citadel Climb
- Hook: `Climb faster than the panic floor rises.`
- Selling points:
  - Easy one-axis onboarding
  - Route optimization through moving platforms
  - Strong replay loop via vertical distance targets

### Node Hopper Drift
- Hook: `Fuel, drift, survive.`
- Selling points:
  - River-survival tension and smooth steering
  - Combo scoring tied to pickup rhythm
  - Distinct pace from shooter/platformer titles

## Campaign Timeline (4 Weeks)
1. Week 1: Teaser
- Drop key art posters + logo reveal
- 10-second gameplay loop clips
- CTA: `Wishlist + follow for demo drop`

2. Week 2: Feature Reveal
- Meme mechanic spotlights (`HODL`, `Fork Panic`, `To The Moon`)
- Dev diary thread with pixel-art process
- Community poll for favorite box art

3. Week 3: Demo + Creator Push
- Release short playable demo builds
- Provide streamer kit: overlays, challenge rules, prize badges
- Launch high-score challenge hashtag
- Run `WCN Signal Save` weekend score challenge stream format

4. Week 4: Launch Week
- Launch trailer (45-60 sec)
- Daily score challenge with themed prompts
- Bundle offer for all featured titles
