Analysis & Insights

Productivity Pattern: Work occurred in two main phases — a Feb 7–19 burst building the core Curio Cards ecosystem and 1n2.org infrastructure, then a gap, followed by an intense Mar 5–8 sprint building the daily pipeline, data warehouse, and creative projects like Bitcoin Trains.
Token Distribution: Top 4 sessions consumed ~4.2M tokens (~49% of total). The long tail of 20+ smaller sessions accounts for ~1.5M. Building new full-stack apps uses 3–10x more tokens than configuration or debugging.
Project Categories: 12 Curio Cards / NFT tools, 7 content aggregators, 5 dashboards, 4 games, 4 AI/automation tools, 3 creative projects, 5 infrastructure/config sessions.
Stack Evolution: Started with PHP/MySQL (MediaLog), shifted to Python + SQLite (Data Labs), then HTML/JS canvas (Bitcoin Trains). The collaboration evolved from "build me an app" to "build me an ecosystem with automated daily pipelines."
