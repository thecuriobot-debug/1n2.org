Sometimes, you’ve gotta give the people exactly what they asked for. After months of fancasting and rumors, Disney confirmed that Kathryn Hahn will step into Mother Gothel’s crimson velvet robe for its live-action Tangled adaptation. Hahn confirmed her casting with an Instagram video wherein she explained the concept of outfit of the day and revealed herself to be wearing a Mother Gothel 90s-inspired T-shirt.
