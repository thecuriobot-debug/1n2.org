Hailey Bieber has been at the forefront of headlines over the years. The star is not new to public scrutiny, backlash, and comments; however, a life under the constant limelight has often caught up to her. In a recent interview, she spilled the beans on how, even through all of this, she can lean on the support of her husband

Justin Bieber, and how he has helped her deal with such situations over the years.

Hailey Bieber reveals how Justin Bieber helped her in dealing with hate comments

While talking to Vogue Australia about having a very publicized life, Hailey shared that having Justin by her side has been of great support over the years. While talking about how she deals with online comments, she shared that since Bieber has been at the forefront of receiving such comments from a very young age, he has helped her a lot in dealing with such things.

She further cited that it has been helpful as he firsthand understands what she goes through because of certain things and comments that are made about her in public. While talking about her time in the limelight as a celebrity, Hailey added that “I think it doesn’t ever really get easier, if I’m being honest. I think it’s always difficult to be dealing with so much noise and so much outside opinion and perspective and constantly being perceived all the time.

”

Hailey Bieber shares that her friends also help her in dealing with the hate

In the interview, the model and entrepreneur also mentioned that the comments remain hurtful, even though you get used to them after a while. Referring to her close friends

Kendall Jenner and Kylie Jenner, she shared that she has friends and other people in her circle who share similar circumstances. “Talking to anybody who has that understanding is always comforting because it’s really hard for anyone else who never experienced it to understand how it really feels,” she added.

On the work front, Hailey Bieber is focusing on working for her beauty brand and remaining involved in the internal operations of the products, as well as promoting them to the world as well.
