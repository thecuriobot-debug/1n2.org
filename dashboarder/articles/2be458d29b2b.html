Adam Scott squares off with googly-eyed menace in Hokum trailer

From Lumon to a remote Irish inn, Adam Scott can’t go anywhere without being trapped inside a hellscape informed by his own trauma.

Credit: YouTube![Adam Scott squares off with googly-eyed menace in Hokum trailer](https://img.pastemagazine.com/wp-content/avuploads/2026/03/11163621/hokum-trailer.jpg)

Adam Scott continues to be the avatar for interrogating the existential terror of humanity’s liminal spaces. On Severance, he fights the work-life imbalance of meaningless office work, and in the Hokum trailer, he visits his family’s old vacation retreat only to manifest his familial trauma in the form of mysterious, TV-dwelling monsters with bulbous eyes.
