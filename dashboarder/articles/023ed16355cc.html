In the version of the article initially published, in Fig. 1f, the panel showing 0 dpi proximal colon was inadvertently duplicated as the H&E-stained 4 dpi proximal colon. In Extended Data Fig. 5b, organoids from the “OHT + zVAD-fmk” group were mislabeled as belonging to the OHT group. In Extended Data Fig. 5d, the TUNEL staining image for 8 dpi Setdb1iEC-KO partially overlapped with that of 8 dpi Setdb1iEC-KO shown in Extended Data Fig. 7b. The figures have been corrected in the HTML and PDF versions of the article. The original, incorrect panels are provided in the Supplementary Information to this corrigendum for full transparency. These corrections do not alter the interpretation or conclusions of the study. The authors thank the reader for bringing these errors to their attention, and apologize for any confusion.

Supplementary information is available in the online version of this amendment.
