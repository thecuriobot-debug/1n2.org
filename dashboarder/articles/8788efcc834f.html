Big Thief are hitting the road again this fall. Just a month before embarking on a European and UK tour, the group has detailed a series of North American dates set to kick off in August. Check out a full itinerary below.

The group—now a trio of Adrianne Lenker, Buck Meek, and James Krivchenia—will begin the run on August 2 in Burlington, Vermont, before making stops in Toronto, Detroit, Chicago, Minneapolis, Nashville, Phoenix, Seattle, and more. The tour concludes on October 6 with a show at Denver’s Red Rocks Amphitheatre.

It’s Big Thief’s second North American tour in support of Double Infinity, the album they released last year on 4AD. The band previously brought the album on the road in fall 2025; at the time, it was their first stateside tour since 2023.
