Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about creating with Prism.

What is Prism?

Prism is an AI video generator for short-form content. Create viral videos for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts from simple text prompts—no editing skills required.

What AI models does Prism use?

Prism integrates the best AI video models including Google Veo, Kling, Sora, Hailuo, Flux, and more. You can switch between models to find the perfect style for your project.

How does the credit system work?

1 credit = $0.01 of compute. Different models cost different amounts. See pricing.

Can I use Prism for commercial projects?

Yes! All content you create with Prism is yours to use commercially. This includes videos for marketing, social media, client projects, and more.

What video formats can I export?

Prism supports exporting in multiple resolutions up to 4K, with various aspect ratios optimized for different platforms like YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, and more.
