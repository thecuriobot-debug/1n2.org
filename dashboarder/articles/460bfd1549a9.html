After a handful of guest appearances in TV shows and a couple of films, Dan Levy’s big Schitt’s Creek follow-up is almost ready for Netflix. The streamer shared the first trailer for Big Mistakes this morning, which brings Levy back as a TV writer after his writing, directing, and acting Emmy wins for the final season of Schitt’s Creek. He stars here as some sort of preacher, with Taylor Ortega playing his sister. Both of them end up involved with an organized crime syndicate and seem to take the situation about as seriously as you’d expect.
