Prabhat Roy returns to direction with short film adaptation of Tagore’s Bolai

Roy said he chose to adapt Bolai because it is an emotionally relatable story that generations of Bengali readers have grown up with. At 82, and after a gap of more than a decade since his last film, the director described the project as deeply personal. Due to health considerations, he has chosen to make it as a short film rather than a full-length feature, calling the opportunity to return to filmmaking once again one of the most meaningful moments of his life.
