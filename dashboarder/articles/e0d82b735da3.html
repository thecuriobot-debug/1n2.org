YouTube now allows politicians, journalists to request removal of AI deepfakes

The video platform is also exploring "opportunities for creators and artists to manage, authorize, and benefit from AI likeness."

Photo by Cheng Xin/Getty Images![YouTube now allows politicians, journalists to request removal of AI deepfakes](https://img.pastemagazine.com/wp-content/avuploads/2025/09/29201942/youtube-feature.jpg)

About six months ago, YouTube unveiled a “likeness detection tool,” which helped a select group of creators to search for AI deepfakes of themselves on the site. Today, YouTube announced that it had expanded this tool to “a pilot group of government officials, journalists, and political candidates,” according to a blog post shared by the site.
