With over 70 million record sales worldwide, Ricky Martin is one of the most commercially successful Latin artists of all time. The Puerto Rican singer started his musical journey at a very young age. Here’s a look at his career milestones.

Ricky Martin’s career: From boy band icon to solo success

Ricky Martin rose to fame in the 1980s as a member of the boy band Menudo. Martin was one of the most recognizable members of the group. After leaving the band, Martin shifted his focus to a solo career, initially recording Spanish-language albums and pursuing acting work.

In the early 1990s, Martin established himself as a major Latin pop star with albums such as ‘Ricky Martin,’ ‘Me Amarás,’ and ‘A Medio Vivir.’

Ricky’s 1999 song ‘Livin’ la Vida Loca’ became a cultural event, and the song topped charts worldwide. It is considered that this song paved the way for Latin music to enter the mainstream.

Ricky Martin’s real estate profile

In November 2016, Ricky paid USD 13.5 million for a home in Beverly Hills, California. At the same time, he listed a New York City apartment that he bought in 2012 for USD 6 million, for USD 8.4 million. He previously owned a home in Beverly Hills, which he sold in 2006 for USD 15 million, and a different NYC condo, which he sold in 2015 for USD 7.55 million.

He also owns a villa in Madrid, a private island off the coast of Rio de Janeiro, and a large property in his home country of Puerto Rico.
