Kelly Clarkson says American Idol still owes her a car

Clarkson issued a grim warning that Traitors champ Rob Rausch may never see his prize money.

Photo by: Weiss Eubanks/NBCUniversal![Kelly Clarkson says American Idol still owes her a car](https://img.pastemagazine.com/wp-content/avuploads/2026/02/02202342/kelly-clarkson-feature.jpg)

Winning the first season of American Idol undeniably changed Kelly Clarkson’s life forever. She’s a huge star with multiple hits and a talk show (for the time being, at least). But one thing she didn’t get from the show was a car, despite being promised one. This betrayal clearly still weighs on her, given the manner in which she discussed it on The Kelly Clarkson Show yesterday. Recent The Traitors winner Rob Rausch was on the show when the topic of his prize money—about $220,000—came up. When Rausch said he hadn’t actually received the money yet, Clarkson unlocked a memory that she had successfully kept private for more than two decades.
