Determined to never hear the stunned silence of a Jo Koy monologue again, the Golden Globes is sticking with what works. Earlier today, the Globes announced that Nikki Glaser will continue her just-mean-enough hot streak for a third time as the host of the 2027 Golden Globes. Should the world continue spinning and the Globes not descend into another huge, embarrassing controversy, Glaser will take the stage to bite the hand that feeds her on January 10, 2027.
