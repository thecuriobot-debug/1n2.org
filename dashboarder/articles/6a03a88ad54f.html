What’s left in Iran ‘could be taken out in an hour’, says Trump

President Trump has told reporters that the US has destroyed Iran’s navy, anti-aircraft systems, radars and leadership. “And we could do a lot worse,” he said, adding what is left standing in the country could be taken out “in an hour”.
