Sonal Chauhan recently attended the India vs. New Zealand T20 World Cup final in Ahmedabad. While everyone enjoyed the thrilling match, the actress experienced a moment she would never forget. She dropped a video of the same on her Instagram and called it "special."

Fans sing 'Zara Sa' for Sonal Chauhan

Sonal Chauhan took to her Instagram handle to share a video of fans who had attended the T20 World Cup final in Ahmedabad. In the clip, they can be seen singing her iconic song ' Zara Sa Dil Mein De Jagah Tu', after spotting her in the VIP stands. The clip showcased Sonal clearly touched by the gesture, and smiling warmly, while waving back at the crowd and recording the moment on her phone.

She captioned the post, "This was so soooo special. Couldn’t capture the whole moment. But it was just too special the way you all sang my song ZARA SA…. I’m sure my smile says it all. Thank you Ahmedabad. I love you Gujrat. I LOVE YOU ALL. Thank you for making me smile always."

More about Sonal Chauhan

Sonal Chauhan marked her debut opposite Emraan Hashmi in the 2008 romantic thriller 'Jannat', directed by Kunal Deshmukh. The film became a hit, especially for its music.

The 'Zara Sa Dil Mein De Jagah Tu' song from the film went on to become a chartbuster and is popular even today on social media. Crooned by KK and composed by Pritam, the song was penned by Sayeed Quadri.

Recently, Sonal Chauhan was in the news when she appealed to PM Narendra Modi seeking help after being stranded in Dubai amidst the ongoing conflict in the Middle East. Later, the actress announced that she is safe and urged everyone not to panic.

On the work front, the actress was last seen in the film 'Adipurush'. She played the role of Mandodari in the Om Raut directoria. It was released in the year 2023.
