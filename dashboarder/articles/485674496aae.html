Beabadoobee has tapped the Marías for a new single. “All I Did Was Dream of You” is Bea Kristi’s first original music since the release of her Rick Rubin-produced 2024 album, This Is How Tomorrow Moves. Jake Erland and Aboveground directed the accompanying music video, which was shot in Vilnius, Lithuania. Watch it below.

Earlier this month, Beabadoobee covered Elliot Smith’s “Say Yes” for War Child’s Help(2) benefit compilation. The Marías were nominated for Best New Artist at this year’s Grammy Awards, though they lost out to Olivia Dean.
