The cryptocurrency market moves quickly, and new digital assets appear regularly as developers experiment with different blockchain technologies and economic models. While established cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin and Ethereum continue to dominate the market, many investors are also paying attention to smaller and emerging tokens that may offer new use cases or growth potential. One of the projects that has started to attract curiosity in crypto communities is SRP cryptocurrency, a token that has gradually entered discussions among traders exploring early-stage altcoins.

For investors, discovering a new cryptocurrency is only the first step. Evaluating its long-term potential requires looking beyond hype and examining key factors such as tokenomics, historical price performance, trading activity, and broader market trends. Technical indicators and overall market sentiment can also provide useful signals about how a token may perform in the short and long term. In this article, we will explore what SRP cryptocurrency is, analyze its market fundamentals and price history, review technical indicators, and examine possible future trends and risks for investors.

What Is SRP Cryptocurrency?

SRP Coin is the native token of Starpad, a platform described as an “all-in-one decentralized NFT gaming platform.” The project aims to bring together several blockchain-based services within a single ecosystem, allowing users to interact with NFT games, digital assets, and launchpad opportunities. Within this system, SRP acts as the core utility token that supports transactions, platform participation, and access to certain services offered by the Starpad ecosystem.

The platform focuses on the growing intersection of blockchain technology, gaming, and non-fungible tokens (NFTs). By combining these elements, Starpad aims to create an environment where developers can launch gaming projects, and users can discover, trade, and interact with blockchain-based assets. As the native token of the platform, SRP can be used for functions such as ecosystem payments, participation in platform features, and other activities designed to support the operation and growth of the Starpad network.

SRP Tokenomics and Market Fundamentals

Understanding a cryptocurrency’s tokenomics helps explain how the asset functions within its ecosystem and how it may behave in the market. Tokenomics includes factors such as supply, distribution, market capitalization, and token utility. For SRP, these elements provide insight into its role within the Starpad platform and its potential market dynamics.

Total Supply and Circulating Supply

Supply metrics are key indicators when analyzing any cryptocurrency. SRP has a total and maximum supply of 680 million tokens, meaning the supply is capped and no additional tokens are expected to be created.

Currently, the self-reported circulating supply is about 30.48 million SRP, representing the portion available for trading and active use. The remaining tokens may be reserved for ecosystem development, incentives, team allocations, or future distribution according to the project’s token schedule.

Market Capitalization

Market capitalization is calculated by multiplying the token’s price by its circulating supply and is commonly used to estimate the size of a crypto project. Because SRP has a relatively small circulating supply, its market cap can change noticeably as demand shifts or more tokens enter circulation.

Another important metric is daily trading volume, which reflects market activity. Higher trading volume generally indicates stronger liquidity, while lower volume may signal limited trading interest.

Utility of the SRP Token

The SRP token functions as the main utility token within the Starpad ecosystem. It can be used for activities such as transactions, participation in launchpad events, and accessing certain platform features.

Within the platform’s NFT gaming environment, SRP may also support interactions with blockchain gaming projects and other ecosystem services, helping facilitate activity within the network.

SRP Cryptocurrency Price History

Analyzing SRP’s historical price performance shows how the token has reacted to different crypto market cycles. Like many smaller altcoins launched during the 2021 crypto boom, SRP experienced a rapid price surge early on, followed by a sharp decline during the broader market downturn. These movements reflect both speculative trading activity and the volatility commonly seen in early-stage blockchain projects.

Initial Launch Price

SRP’s public sale began in late 2021, with an Initial Exchange Offering (IEO) on Gate.io on September 24, 2021, priced at about $0.023 per token. Earlier seed and IDO rounds were reportedly offered at similar prices.

This $0.023 launch price became the main reference point for early investors. During the 2021 bull market, the token surged significantly, allowing early participants to see large paper gains before the market later corrected.

Major Price Milestones

SRP reached its all-time high (ATH) on November 25, 2021, when the project was still known as Starpunk before rebranding to Starpad. Different data sources report slightly different peak prices, ranging from about $0.365 to around $0.92. Regardless of the exact figure, the token once traded hundreds of times higher than its current value.

After the peak, SRP’s price fell sharply during the crypto bear market. By May 2025, the token reached an all-time low (ATL) of about $0.000063, representing a drop of more than 99.9% from its peak. The token also experienced strong volatility along the way, including a 99.46% drop in 2022, followed by a 61% rebound in 2023, although it largely stayed below $0.004 during 2023–2024.

Recent Market Performance

During 2024–2025, SRP has traded close to its historical lows with limited market activity. Some analyses place the token’s recent baseline price around $0.000162, indicating that it now trades at only a small fraction of its previous peak value.

SRP Cryptocurrency Technical Price Analysis

SRP’s relatively low liquidity makes technical analysis less reliable compared with larger cryptocurrencies. With limited trading volume, price charts often appear flat, and indicators can produce weaker signals. However, it is still possible to identify general support and resistance zones as well as some basic momentum indicators.

Key Support and Resistance Levels

The main support level for SRP is close to its all-time low around $0.00006, where the price has rarely moved lower. This level may attract some buying interest if the token approaches it again.

On the upside, resistance levels appear around $0.0002–$0.0003, which correspond to recent minor rebound highs. Because SRP has traded near its historical lows for a long period, these support and resistance zones remain relatively weak.

Moving Averages and Market Indicators

On the weekly chart, SRP is trading below most moving averages, reflecting the long-term downtrend after the 2021 peak.

Short-term indicators show mixed signals. The Relative Strength Index (RSI) remains in the neutral range (30–70), suggesting the asset is neither overbought nor oversold. Meanwhile, the MACD indicator recently produced a bullish crossover, which may signal a possible short-term price uptick. However, due to the token’s low trading activity, these signals should be interpreted cautiously.

Short-Term Market Sentiment

Overall, short-term sentiment toward SRP appears largely neutral. Most technical indicators do not show strong buy or sell signals, and trading volume remains low. While some traders view the MACD crossover as a potential bullish sign, the lack of strong market activity suggests that SRP currently has limited short-term momentum.

Market Trends Influencing SRP Cryptocurrency

The price performance of smaller altcoins like SRP is often shaped by broader market forces rather than project developments alone. Several external factors can influence its price movement and investor interest.

-

Overall crypto market trends: The performance of major cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin and Ethereum often affects smaller altcoins. When the market enters a bullish phase, investors may shift funds into smaller tokens, while bearish conditions typically reduce demand for higher-risk assets.

-

Bitcoin and altcoin market cycles: Altcoins often follow a cycle where capital first flows into large-cap assets and later moves into smaller projects. SRP’s price movements may reflect these broader altcoin cycles.

-

Investor sentiment: Market optimism or uncertainty can strongly affect trading activity. In positive market conditions, traders may explore emerging projects like blockchain gaming tokens, while risk-off periods usually reduce interest in smaller assets.

-

Adoption and partnerships: Growth in the NFT and blockchain gaming sector could influence interest in platforms like Starpad. Increased ecosystem development or partnerships may support demand for the SRP token.

-

Regulatory environment: Changes in cryptocurrency regulations, exchange policies, or digital asset rules can impact accessibility and trading activity, particularly for smaller tokens with limited market presence.

SRP Cryptocurrency Price Prediction

Predicting the price of smaller cryptocurrencies like SRP is challenging because of low liquidity and limited market activity. Its future performance will likely depend on overall crypto market cycles, the growth of the Starpad ecosystem, and investor interest in blockchain gaming and NFT platforms.

Short-Term Prediction (2026–2027)

In the short term, SRP is expected to remain near its current low price range, around $0.00016–$0.00020, unless trading activity increases. A stronger crypto market could lead to a small rebound, while low liquidity may keep the price moving sideways.
