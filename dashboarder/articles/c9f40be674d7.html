Another one of those golf shows gets closer to the green today as Netflix shares the first look at The Hawk, the Will Ferrell-led comedy that we’ve been hearing about since at least last year. Officially titled The Hawk, Ferrell stars as Lonnie “The Hawk” Hawkins. He’s “a legend,” “an icon, a champion, an American hero,” some very dramatic voiceover tells us in the first teaser.
