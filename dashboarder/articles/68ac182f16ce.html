Oscar Week parties and events are in full swing.

Lana Condor, Alexandra Shipp and Jurnee Smollett were some of the bold-faced names who attended Dior’s Club J’adore in Beverly Hills on Monday night. On Tuesday, Tyriq Withers and Maika Monroe walked the red carpet at the premiere of their new Colleen Hoover drama, “Reminders of Him,” in Hollywood. At the same time, “Hacks” star Meg Stalter, Margaret Cho and Mae Martin received special honors at the 14th annual Queerties Awards.

That was Conan O’Brien, who returns as host of the Oscars for the second year in a row, rolling out the red carpet in front of the Dolby Theatre ahead of the big night on Sunday.

Teyana Taylor, Chloé Zhao, Sheryl Lee Ralph and Lucy Liu were among the honorees at Time’s Women of the Year dinner at the West Hollywood Edition hotel.

“I am so nervous,” Taylor, who goes into Oscar night as a nominee for her work in “One Battle After Another,” told me when she arrived at the Time evnt. “I’m realizing this is about to come to an end and I really had a blast. There are all the friendships I made and all the beautiful people that I’ve enjoyed running into all awards season. I’m going to miss everybody. This whole journey has been so beautiful.”

On Wednesday, Diane von Furstenberg hosted her annual luncheon for female Oscar nominees. The guest list included Gwyneth Paltrow, Katy Perry, Jane Fonda, Tracee Ellis Ross, Demi Moore, Tig Notaro, Ruth E. Carter and Autumn Durald Arkapaw, among many others.
