Bodies Bodies Bodies writer to help Jenna Ortega and Taylor Russell go all Single White Female

Pulitzer finalist Sarah DeLappe has been tapped to pen Ortega and Russell's planned remake of the classic '90s roommate thriller.

Photos: Jon Kopaloff/Getty Images for Lionsgate; Troy Harvey/©A.M.P.A.S.![Bodies Bodies Bodies writer to help Jenna Ortega and Taylor Russell go all Single White Female](https://img.pastemagazine.com/wp-content/avuploads/2025/03/14133819/MixCollage-14-Mar-2025-01-30-PM-7657.jpg)

Jenna Ortega and Taylor Russell are reportedly one step closer to getting to Single White Female each other, as Deadline reports that the pair’s planned remake of the 1992 thriller has just secured a writer. Specifically, Sarah DeLappe, the Pulitzer Prize finalist playwright who picked up some film cred with her enjoyably chaotic script for 2022’s Bodies Bodies Bodies, has been attached to the project, which is being set up at the Sony-owned 3000 Pictures studio. The film has also had producers Stacey Sher (Django Unchained) and Marisa Paiva attached (in addition to Ortega and Russell), suggesting that the studio is getting serious about seeing these two leads go head-to-head with a little full-contact cohabitation.
