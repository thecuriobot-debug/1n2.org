Lowcountry Legal Volunteers hosting health fair March 13 in Beaufort

Lowcountry Legal Volunteers will host a Health and Legal Services Fair March 13 at the Lowcountry Outreach Center in Beaufort from 10 a.m. until 2 p.m. The event is free to the public.

- A free Health and Legal Services Fair will be held on March 14 in Beaufort, South Carolina.

- The event connects residents with various medical, legal, and community service providers.

- Lowcountry Legal Volunteers and Good Neighbor Free Medical Clinic are the primary partners for the fair.

- Free health screenings, Spanish translators, and information on services like expungements will be available.

Citizens around the Lowcountry will be given the opportunity to connect with medical, legal and community service providers March 14 in Beaufort.

Lowcountry Legal Volunteers will be partnering with Good Neighbor Free Medical Clinic and other community organizations for a Health and Legal Services Fair at the Lowcountry Outreach Center from 10 a.m. until 2 p.m. The center is located at 80 Lady’s Island Drive in Beaufort.

Lowcountry Legal Volunteers is a nonprofit organization which provides free civil legal services to low-income residents in Beaufort, Jasper and Hampton counties. Good Neighbor Free Medical Clinic is a provider of free health care to uninsured adults.

The event is free and will bring several agencies together into one place to allow citizens a chance to speak with the various groups. No appointments are required for the event, there will be Spanish translators on site, and there will be free refreshments.

“Access to legal help and access to health care are deeply connected,” Hannah Kidd, medical-legal partnership director and attorney for Lowcountry Legal Volunteers, said. “When people can meet with trusted providers in one place, ask questions, learn what resources are available and start the process of getting help, it removes barriers and creates real opportunities for families to move forward.”

Mari Valentín, an AmeriCorps community health worker at Good Neighbor Free Medical Clinic, said the event is designed to make services easier to navigate.

“When we bring trusted medical and legal partners together in one space, we’re helping neighbors navigate systems that can otherwise feel overwhelming and ensuring they know they are not alone in seeking support,” Valentín recently said.

Organizations participating in the event include Lowcountry Legal Volunteers; Good Neighbor Free Medical Clinic; Lowcountry Outreach Center; Child Abuse Prevention Association (CAPA) Beaufort; Center for Heirs’ Property Preservation; Hopeful Horizons; A Father’s Place; Memory Matters; Beaufort Memorial Hospital; South Carolina Legal Services; Med-I-Assist; SC Thrive; Nexus Care of Beaufort County; Beaufort County Library; and the 14th Circuit Solicitor’s Office, which will provide information about expungements and its SAFE Program.

Beaufort Memorial Hospital will also be offering free blood pressure and glucose screenings, as well as $10 cholesterol panels and $10 PSA tests for men. Payment for the low-cost services must be made by cash or check.

For more information about the event, contact Mari Valentín at 843-470-9088 or Hannah Kidd at 843-815-1570.
