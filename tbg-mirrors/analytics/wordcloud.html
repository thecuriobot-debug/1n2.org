📖 Short Story: "Episode 500"
Thomas checked the price one more time before hitting the stream button. Bitcoin had done something interesting again — it always did. For over a decade, every week, the same question: higher or lower? The guests had changed. The topics had evolved from Silk Road to ETFs, from mining rigs in garages to nation-state reserves. But the core never moved. The network kept producing blocks. The show kept going. He shook the Magic 8-Ball. "Without a doubt," it said. He smiled. The 8-Ball was wrong about half the time, but it was always confident. That counted for something. "Welcome to The Bitcoin Group," he said into the microphone. "The American original."
