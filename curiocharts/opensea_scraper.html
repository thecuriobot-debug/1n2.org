What's Wrong with CoinGecko?

CoinGecko shows 0.051 ETH floor, but OpenSea shows 0.093 ETH.

Why the difference?

  • CoinGecko aggregates from multiple marketplaces (including lower-tier ones)
  • OpenSea is the primary marketplace for Curio Cards
  • CoinGecko data may be delayed or include outliers
  • Individual cards have different floors (0.093 to 0.88 ETH)
