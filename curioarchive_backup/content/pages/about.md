## About Curio Archive
Curio Archive is an independent historical encyclopedia documenting the on-chain history of Curio Cards (2017).

Its purpose is preservation, not promotion.

## Methodology
- Primary records are prioritized over secondary summaries.
- Claims are attributed to source category.
- Missing records are not inferred.

## Data Handling
- All entries are generated from structured blockchain data and updated regularly.
- Markdown content provides editorial narrative.
- JSON datasets provide structured record inputs.
- Generated pages preserve schema order and timestamped publication output.

## Record Intent
The archive seeks to provide a permanent, neutral record of one of the earliest Ethereum-based NFT collections.
