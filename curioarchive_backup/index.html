Interpretive Notes (Clearly Marked)

Curio Cards is frequently described in both project documentation and external reporting as one of the earliest Ethereum-native art NFT collections. This archive treats that framing as a historically relevant description, while preserving source attribution because terminology in early blockchain history can vary by source and definition.
