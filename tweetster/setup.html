How It Works

  1. Your credentials are saved securely on your DigitalOcean droplet
  2. Twikit (Python) logs into Twitter using your account (saves cookies)
  3. Fetches tweets from your home timeline or specific accounts
  4. Tweets are classified by topic (Bitcoin, Sports, Tech, Politics)
  5. Results saved to data/tweets.json for the Tweetster UI

⚠️ Note: Twitter may occasionally require re-authentication. If fetches fail, re-run the Login step.
